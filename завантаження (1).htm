Our “mini-BLOXcon” at Maker Faire Bay Area 2013 was a resounding success – so much so that we took home an “Editors Choice” award for our booth! Hundreds of Maker Faire attendees swung through to learn about ROBLOX and our community of builders, experience playing and building first-hand, learn more about dynamic lighting, and pick up some sweet ROBLOX merchandise. To see photos and video from the event, check out our Maker Faire page.

BLOXcon is the quintessential gathering of ROBLOX staff and fans, where building, playing, learning and making friends are the priorities of the day. As a BLOXcon attendee, you'll get exclusive access to new ROBLOX features, meet fellow ROBLOXians, see presentations by staff and veteran users, participate in interactive events and more!

This summer, for the first time, ROBLOX will be hosting BLOXcon away from home. We'll be visiting Chicago, London and New York City, and each city will play host to a unique, tailored program of activities and presentations. We can't wait to meet more of our passionate users from around the world!

We will continue to update this page with additional details in the near future. You can stay up to date by following @BLOXcon and tracking #BLOXcon on Twitter, and keeping your eye on the ROBLOX blog.
